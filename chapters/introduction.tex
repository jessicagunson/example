Animals communicate via complex, multi-component, multi-modal signals that include visual, auditory, olfactory, and tactile components \parencite{fröhlichFunctionPrimateMultimodal2018}. Many colorful and behavioral signals displayed across the animal kingdom evolved as adaptations for female mate choice \parencite{anderssonFemaleChoiceSelects1982,darwinSexualSelectionDescent1871,hillBirdColorationFunction2006}.
